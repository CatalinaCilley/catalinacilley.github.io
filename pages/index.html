About Rev. Catalina Cilley

I am an ordained minister committed to providing compassionate and inclusive spiritual services. Ordained by American Marriage Ministries, my mission is to create meaningful and memorable experiences for individuals and families during life's most significant moments.

Whether you are celebrating a joyous occasion, seeking comfort during a difficult time, or simply looking for spiritual guidance, I am here to support you. My approach is rooted in respect, empathy, and a deep understanding of diverse spiritual paths.
